## SCENARIO 1: EXCEPTIONAL PROGRESS (10%)
### Narrative Structure: Achievement-focused, positive reinforcement, goal attainment

**Template:**
```
Session Date: {session_date}
Client: {client_name}
Staff: {staff_name}, {credential}
Location: {location}
Session Duration: {duration} minutes

EXCEPTIONAL PROGRESS SESSION

{client_name} demonstrated outstanding progress during today's session at {location}. 
{client_name} achieved {goal_type} goal (STO{goal_number}) {timing} ahead of schedule, 
marking significant advancement in {skill_area}.

Throughout the session from {start_time} to {end_time}, {client_name} consistently 
exhibited {positive_behavior_1}, {positive_behavior_2}, and {positive_behavior_3}. 
{pronoun_subject} completed all scheduled activities independently with 95% accuracy.

{parent_notification}
{parent_first_name} {client_last_name} expressed pride in {client_name}'s achievements 
during {contact_method} at {contact_time}.

STAFF OBSERVATIONS:
- Mastered {specific_skill} with minimal prompting
- Generalized skills across multiple settings ({location_1}, {location_2})
- Positive peer interactions with {peer_name} during group activity

RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Continue current intervention strategies
- Advance to next skill level in {program_area}
- Schedule review with {provider_name} on {followup_date}

Session documented by: {staff_name}, {credential}
{signature_block}
```

**PHI Variations:**
- Client names: 4 formats (First Last, Last First, Last First MI, First MI Last)
- Parent names: Both parents mentioned 40% of time
- Dates: Achievement dates, session dates, follow-up dates
- Times: Specific session start/end times
- Staff credentials: BCBA, M.A., Behavior Specialist II
- Locations: 2-3 locations mentioned (generalization context)
