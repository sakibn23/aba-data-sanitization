**Operational Definition:** Liam swearing, making threats (e.g., threatening to hurt others, damage property, etc.), and/or using vulgar language

**Frequency:** 4 instances
**Duration:** Total approximately 12 minutes (Average: 3 minutes per instance)
**Intensity:** Moderate

- Being told 'no' (denied continued access to YouTube at 9:15 AM)
- Transition from preferred activity (watching videos) to non-preferred activity (hygiene tasks)

**Warning Signs Observed:** Yes - Liam's voice volume increased, he moved away from staff, crossed his arms, and made angry facial expressions before engaging in verbal aggression. Staff recognized these signs and attempted early intervention, but Liam's response was delayed today.

**Restrictive Interventions Used:** None
