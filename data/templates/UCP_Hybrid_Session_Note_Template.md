## TEMPLATE USAGE NOTES

### UCP-Specific Terminology to Use:
- "Person we support" or client's first name (not "client")
- "Challenging behaviors" (not "problem behaviors")
- "SCIP-R" techniques (their crisis intervention method)
- "Individualized Universal Protocol"
- "Synthesized reinforcers"
- "Residential Program" and "Day Program" (their program types)

### UCP Program Names to Use:
- **Residential Programs:** [Number] Geiger ICF (e.g., "92 Geiger ICF", "88 Geiger ICF")
- **Day Programs:** Chadwicks TEC, Liverpool TEC, Other TEC locations

### Common UCP Staff Titles:
- Behavior Specialist II
- Behavior Specialist
- BCBA (Board Certified Behavior Analyst)
- Sr. BCBA
- RBT (Registered Behavior Technician)
- Direct Support Professional (DSP)

### Data Collection Frequencies:
- "Per INSTANCE" (most common for challenging behaviors)
- "Per OCCURRENCE"
- "FREQUENCY count"
- "DURATION in minutes/seconds"
- "INTENSITY rating" (Low/Moderate/High)

### Length Guidelines:
- **Typical session note:** 2-3 pages
- **Note with challenging behaviors:** 3-4 pages
- **Routine session, no incidents:** 1-2 pages
